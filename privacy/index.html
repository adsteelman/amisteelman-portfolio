Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

I build small tools and apps as an independent developer. This policy covers all apps and add-ons published by Ami Steelman at amisteelman.com. I keep things simple: your data stays yours, and I don't collect what I don't need.

Who this covers

This policy applies to all apps, add-ons, and tools published by Ami Steelman, including:

  • Google Workspace FontSwap — font scanning and replacement for Google Slides & Docs
  • Any future apps or tools published under amisteelman.com

What my apps access

Each app only requests access to what it genuinely needs to function. For Google Workspace add-ons like FontSwap:

  • Font family names and text style metadata within your open document
  • The ability to modify text styles in your document (to perform replacements)
  • Your document's UI context (to display the add-on dialog)

This access is used solely to perform the operation you request — scanning or replacing fonts — within your current session.

What I do not do

  • I do not store, log, or retain any of your document content
  • I do not transmit your data to any external server
  • I do not sell, share, or trade your data with third parties
  • I do not use your data for advertising or analytics
  • I do not retain any information between sessions

All processing happens locally within Google's infrastructure during your active session. When you close the add-on, nothing is kept.

Third-party services

My Google Workspace add-ons run entirely within Google's Apps Script platform and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. I do not integrate any additional third-party analytics, tracking, or data services.

Children's privacy

My apps are not directed at children under 13 and I do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If I add new apps that handle data differently, I will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of my apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
